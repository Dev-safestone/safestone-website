Just like any other social networking platform
like LinkedIn or Instagram, you can connect with
your friends, family, colleagues, neighbours, etc.
on the SafeStone app. The network built
on SafeStone acts as a user's personal safety
network that can be reached out to during emergency.

Users can shortlist close friends and family into
safe circles or safe groups from their safety
network. Members of a Safe Circle can view
each other's location in real time similar to
Snapchat maps.

SafeFeed is a social media-like feed that
shows you updates from your circles, such
as their latest check-ins or status updates.
You can also discover new places to explore
and share them with your circles.

Build your own safety networks Just like any other social networking platform like LinkedIn or Instagram, you can connect with your friends, family, colleagues, neighbours, etc. on the SafeStone app. The network built on SafeStone acts as a user's personal safety network that can be reached out to during emergency.
Create your SafeCircles Users can shortlist close friends and family into safe circles or safe groups from their safety network. Members of a Safe Circle can view each other's location in real time similar to Snapchat maps.
Stay connected with SafeFeed SafeFeed is a social media-like feed that shows you updates from your circles, such as their latest check-ins or status updates. You can also discover new places to explore and share them with your circles.

Our Vision at SafeStone
The vision of our app is to create a safer, more connected world. We believe that technology can be used for good, and we're committed to leveraging it to make a positive impact on society. Our vision is to create a platform that empowers users to build and maintain strong, safe, and supportive social networks. We aim to foster a community where people can connect with like-minded individuals, share safety tips, and stay up-to-date on the latest safety news. Our ultimate goal is to make the world a safer and more enjoyable place for everyone.
